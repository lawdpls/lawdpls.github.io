Scalable Power User Assignment

A brand new data-driven approach to optimize the utilization of power stations and minimize transmission losses.

What is SPUA

SPUA is a user assignment scheme that optimizes the transmission losses and power station utilizations. Moreover, it scales with the size of data, tens of millions of power users can be assigned in one go!
