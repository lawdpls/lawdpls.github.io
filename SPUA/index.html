Scalable Power User Assignment Introduction

Urbanization

Urbanization has seen some rapid growth over the years all around the globe.

Power Consumption

As more and more population shifts its focus to the urban area, power consumption is skyrocketing in an acute fashion.

To accomodate the needs for energy, we have to consider several factors besides merely generate more.


Resource Depletion

Resources to generate energy in a traditional way is limited.

Renewable Sources Not Making Impact

Depletable sources are still the backbone of energy generation.

Therefore, we can actually "generate" more by saving more!


If power users can be assigned to the substations optimally, we not only can minimize transmission losses, but also maximize the utilization of substations.

Current Assignment


Take a look at the current power assignment of Xinjiang provence, China. The problem is two-fold.

Long Distance Coverage

The long distance ensures a very high transmission loss.

Uneven Utilization

Some substations are near their capacities while others are merely used.
